How it works

Just follow this steps and watch movies with your friends for free!

Download the extension

Download and unzip this file and follow the instructions in this link for adding the extension.

Open a video in PrimeVideo

Go to Prime video, log in to your account and choose the movie or show you want to watch.


Create a party

Click on the popcorn icon located next to the address bar. Then click "Start Party" and share the link with your friends.

Join a party

Use the party link and click the popcorn icon next to the address bar. Select a name and click "Join".
